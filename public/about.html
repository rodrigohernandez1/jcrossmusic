Bio

J Cross is a Mexican producer, audio engineer, and DJ born in Mexico City, with over six years of experience in the music industry in Miami. Known for his versatility and innovative vision, he has established himself as Farruko’s official producer, standing out for fusing electronic and urban sounds with a distinctive, forward-thinking style.

As a producer and audio engineer, he has earned 10 platinum records in the United States and received a Grammy nomination for Best Urban Album in 2022. He has also achieved two No. 1 songs on the Billboard charts, and his music catalog has surpassed 1 Billion digital streams across global platforms.

Throughout his career, he has created multi-platinum RIIA certified records across genres such as reggaeton, trap, electronic music, and more, showcasing a strong ability to adapt and evolve within modern music.

He has collaborated with and shared the stage alongside artists such as Farruko, Víctor Mendivil, Jhay Cortez, Arcángel, Darell, Dalex, Marshmello, Oscar Maydon, El Malilla, Elvis Crespo, and Austin Millz. His music has taken him to perform at venues in major U.S. cities including Los Angeles, New York, Boston, Chicago, Austin, San Diego, and Miami, as well as Europe, where he performed alongside Farruko in Amsterdam.
